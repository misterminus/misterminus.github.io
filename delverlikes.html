    What is a Delverlike? A delverlike is a game where you are usually spawned into an Elevator or something similar, and you usually have buttons or tasks to click or complete. Upon completion of all tasks, you return back to the Elevator and progress to the next level. Most commonly there are monsters that guard each level.
    About This list contains the Delverlike games the Japey community has found. If you would like to suggest a game to be added, please speak up in the Japey discord, or DM @bontboss or @doggiedadev. This page is dedicated to archiving the genre, by listing games the community has knowledge of. List is up to date as of 02/09/2025
    Addition Abstraction - Nyansu A rogue-like experience about delving into the deep depths of a dangerous facility. Various threats will attempt to take you down on every floor. Private servers are free, and this game is best played with friends. Expect a sharp difficulty curve that you will need to overcome to dive deeper into the heart of the game! Each death is a learning experience.

You and your team must descend into a network of underground caverns that have been corrupted by a malevolent force by repairing generators, all while sneaking past the evil forces that patrol the place, gathering weapons and items to help you defend yourself if you're spotted. How low can you go?
